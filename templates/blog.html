Data Science with Healthcare: Unveiling Our Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App with Flask and Machine Learning Technology

In today's tech-driven world, access to prompt and accurate healthcare information is more crucial than ever. As remote healthcare solutions gain prominence, we've embarked on an exciting journey to develop a cutting-edge symptom-based disease diagnosis web application. By harnessing the power of Flask for the backend and a Decision Tree Classifier model, we've created an intuitive platform that enables users to swiftly identify potential illnesses based on their reported symptoms.

The Challenge

Our project started with a fundamental realization: people frequently experience symptoms and seek rapid insights into their health concerns. Differentiating between various diseases, especially when symptoms overlap, can be daunting. Our mission was to provide a seamless solution that allows users to input their symptoms and receive potential diagnoses quickly.

The Solution

We've crafted a web app that empowers users to enter a list of symptoms they are experiencing. The app employs a pre-trained Decision Tree Classifier model to predict the most likely disease based on these symptoms. Here's how it unfolds:

  1. Symptom Input: Users enter their symptoms through a user-friendly interface. The web app supports a wide range of symptoms, making it versatile for different scenarios.
  2. Machine Learning Model: Our Decision Tree Classifier, trained on a dataset of symptoms and corresponding diseases, predicts potential diseases based on user inputs. The model efficiently handles variations in symptom synonyms.
  3. Prediction: Leveraging the model, the app provides users with instant predictions of the most likely disease, delivering valuable insights into potential health concerns.
  4. Additional Information: To enrich the user experience, the app offers detailed information about the predicted disease. This includes descriptions, recommended precautions, medications, dietary advice, and workout tips.
  5. Speech Recognition: For added convenience, the app features speech recognition, allowing users to input symptoms verbally.
  6. User Records: Users can enter their name, age, and location to maintain records and personalize their experience. This feature aids in effective data management and tracking.

Key Features

  • User-Friendly Interface: The web app boasts an intuitive and easy-to-navigate interface, ensuring that users can input their symptoms without any hassle.
  • Case-Insensitive and Synonym-Supported Input: The app handles case-insensitive inputs and recognizes synonyms for symptoms, making it versatile and user-friendly.
  • Precise Predictions: Powered by machine learning, our model delivers accurate disease predictions based on user-provided symptoms.
